SAMI MathsClub App

SAMI Maths Club is a collection of mathematical problems and puzzles to support mathematical thinking, problem solving, and a love of mathematics!

All problems come with a full set of facilitator notes so that the club can be either student or teacher-led, offering not only solutions but also suggested teaching strategies and extension activities.
