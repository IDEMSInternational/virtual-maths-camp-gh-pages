Pause-and-Go Videos

Call for contribution: share your passion for teaching science, submit a maths session video for the Africa Virtual Maths Camp 2020!

Do you love the idea of teaching and communicating science? Would you like to give your contribution to an African Maths Camp but you did not have yet the possibility to go in person? We open a call for passionate teachers and communicators in the form of short videos on accessible topics that involve mathematics in a broad sense. Your video will be part of very precious content repository for the virtual maths camp for this year and the future ones!

If you'd like to contribute a video please read the Guidelines for Contributions and email us a link. Many thanks in advance!
