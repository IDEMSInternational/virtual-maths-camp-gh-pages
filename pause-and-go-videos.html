Pause-and-Go Videos

Dear facilitators and learners,

Welcome to the video sessions! We designed these sessions on the format of usual maths camp sessions, with many planned pauses "stop-and-go‚Äù for the learners to think, discuss, experience and experiment, and for the learners to guide them on the side.

Each video corresponds to a session of about 45min, with the video lasting about 10min, and the remaining 35min are for you to be protagonists!

Please actively pause the videos at every planned pause and take the time you need! Every video has two attachments:

  • The facilitators guidelines: these explain the material needed for the session, contain a detailed plan of the session, and solutions for the facilitators to read in advance.
  • Subtitles: either to support the audio in case some sentences are not clear, or for the translation in different languages.
